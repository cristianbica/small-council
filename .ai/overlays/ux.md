# Overlay: ux

Purpose:
- Keep outputs clear, usable, and consistent for humans.
- Reduce friction in common and edge-case user flows.

When to apply:
- User-facing content, workflows, prompts, and documentation.
- UI behavior changes, copy updates, and interaction design decisions.

Output focus:
- Simplicity, clarity, and predictable structure.
- Accessibility-aware decisions (labels, states, navigation).
- Minimal cognitive load for first-time and repeat users.

Decision prompts:
- What is the user trying to do right now?
- Where could confusion, ambiguity, or extra effort occur?
- Is the wording/action sequence obvious without extra explanation?

Quality checks:
- Interaction steps are concise and logically ordered.
- Error/empty/loading states are considered where relevant.
- Terminology is consistent with the rest of the product.
