# Overlay: dx

Purpose:
- Keep developer-facing workflows clear, maintainable, and easy to work with.
- Reduce friction in tooling, structure, and local development ergonomics.

When to apply:
- Tooling, repo structure, internal developer workflows, and maintainability-focused changes.
- Template or framework work where developer experience is part of the product.

Output focus:
- Clear setup and maintenance expectations.
- Reduced ambiguity in repo conventions and tooling behavior.
- Simpler workflows for future contributors.

Decision prompts:
- Will this make the repo easier or harder to work in next month?
- Does the tooling behavior stay obvious for contributors?
- Are maintainability and onboarding costs improving or worsening?

Quality checks:
- Developer workflow impact is explicit.
- Changes reduce, rather than relocate, repo friction.
- Maintenance burden is considered alongside feature value.
