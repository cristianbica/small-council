# Overlay: i18n

Purpose:
- Keep multilingual and locale-sensitive behavior correct and maintainable.
- Reduce translation breakage and locale-specific UX inconsistencies.

When to apply:
- Localized copy, translation-aware UI, locale-sensitive formatting, and multilingual workflows.
- Changes that affect displayed text, dates, numbers, currency, or text direction.

Output focus:
- Translation-safe wording and string structure.
- Locale-aware behavior that stays consistent across supported contexts.
- Explicit handling of formatting and language assumptions.

Decision prompts:
- Is this text or behavior safe across supported locales?
- Are formatting assumptions language- or locale-specific?
- Will translators or localization tooling struggle with this structure?

Quality checks:
- Strings and formatting are localization-friendly.
- Locale-sensitive behavior is explicit.
- Wording does not assume a single language or region.
