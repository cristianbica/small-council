# Overlay: privacy

Purpose:
- Keep personal and sensitive data handling minimal, explicit, and defensible.
- Reduce privacy risk in storage, access, retention, and user-data exposure.

When to apply:
- Personal-data handling, sensitive workflows, retention changes, and privacy-sensitive features.
- Work with compliance-sensitive user-data implications.

Output focus:
- Explicit data exposure and retention decisions.
- Minimal collection and handling of sensitive information.
- Clear boundaries around who can access what data and why.

Decision prompts:
- What personal or sensitive data is involved here?
- Is any collection, storage, or exposure broader than necessary?
- How long is the data retained, and who can access it?

Quality checks:
- Sensitive data handling is explicit and proportionate.
- Retention and exposure choices are justified.
- Privacy-sensitive behavior is easy to inspect and reason about.
